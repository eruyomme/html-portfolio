The Best Movies According to Daiyu

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Annihilation

This one became an instant classic to me. I actually didn't know the term "cosmic horror" until this movie. I love the weird feeling it gives me.

Pan's Labyrinth

A childhood favourite. Loved it then, love it now, gonna love it forever. I am glad I watched it as a child because I used to be such a scaredy cat but once I saw Ophelia being so brave (and I was roughly the same age as her), I decided that I, too, could face my fears. Beautiful folk story, stunning photography, left its mark on me forever.

Midsommar

This is one is relatively new. I'm really obsessed with everything about it... The nature cult, the fact that it never truly gets dark (so you can see the characters getting worn out of this kind of scenery as the days pass by), Dani's crazy resolution, the use of psychedelics, the VISUAL EFFECTS of psychedelics. Yeah, I could ramble about it for days if you allow me too.
